About Us

Fresh Baberz

Fresh Barberz was founded in Stockholm in 2022. We started Fresh Barberz because we wanted to provide a higher level of service than most barber shops offer. Unlike many corporate or franchise barber shops, Fresh Barberz is owned and operated by highly trained barbers who ensure each barber is trained to provide the highest level of service to each client. We work with clients who appreciate the extra time and attention to detail that is hard to find these days. We don't try to be everything to everybody, we specialize in haircuts, beard trims and shaves. Appointments are not required but are highly recommended so we can give you the time you deserve. Our mission is simple, to help our clients look their best.
